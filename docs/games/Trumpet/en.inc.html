A game by Phil Orbanes Sr.

Components

  • 69 game cards:

    • 66 numbered cards: 1-11 in ♠, ♥, ♣, ♦, ✿ & ★.

    • 3 super-trumps: J, Q & 2 Ks in ⎈

  • 6 trump cards: A in ♠, ♥, ♣, ♦, ✿ & ★.

  • 24 path cards:

    • 18 blanks: 3 Ks in ♠, ♥, ♣, ♦, ✿ & ★.

    • 6 wheels: 1-6 in ⎈.

  • 12 character cards: 2 Butterflies, 2 Wolves, 2 Owls, 2 Toads, 2 Turtles & 2 Monkeys.
